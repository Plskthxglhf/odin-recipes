No-Rise Pizza Dough

Perfect crust no-rise Pizza

A quick Pizza dough recipe that involves mixing a few basic ingredients.

A perfect crust every time without needing to wait for the dough to rise.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine flour,sugar,yeast and salt in a large bowl. Mix in warm water and oil until dough comes together.

Cook`s Note

  1. To bake the pizza:
  2. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees C (190 degrees C).
  3. Pat and stretch dough onto a large pizza pan
  4. cover with pizza sauce and top with pepperoni slices and shredded cheese, or desired toppings.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until crust is lightly browned, 20 to 25 minutes.
