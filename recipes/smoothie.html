Peanut Butter Banana Smoothie

Delicious PB and Banana Smoothie

A Easy to make, delicious and quick breakfast, snack or dessert to enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas, broken into chunks
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoon honey, or to taste
  • 2 cups of ice cubes
  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place banana chunks, milk, peanut butter and ice cubes into the blender.
  3. Blend until smooth
  4. Enjoy!
