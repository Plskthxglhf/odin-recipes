Peach and Blueberry Feta Salad

Fresh and sweet summer salad

This peach and blueberry feta salad is perfect for the summer.

Sweet fresh peaches,blueberries and the savory flavor of the feta.

Finished with a basil vinaigrette dressing on top.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of chopped Romaine lettuce
  • 3 fresh peaches - skin on, pitted, and cut into bite-sized pieces, divided
  • 1 cucumber, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries
  • 1/2 cuo crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 shallot
  • 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh basil
  • 1 tablespoon agave or honey, or more to taste

Directions

  1. Put chopped romaine, 2 peaches, cucumbers, and blueberries to a large bowl.Toss well, add feta on top, and set aside.
  2. Add remaining peach, olive oil, shallot, lemon juice, basil, and agave to a food processor. Blend well, taste, and adjust if needed.
  3. Drizzle dressing over salad and mix to combine.
